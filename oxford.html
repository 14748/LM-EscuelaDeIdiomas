OFFICIAL OXFORD EXAMS

Why Choose Our Oxford Official Exams?

Do you need to certify your level of English? Does your university ask you for a certificate? Are you going on Erasmus? Do you want to advance in your professional career? For these or other reasons, it is your moment. The OTE certification exam, which Oxford certifies, is recognized by a long list of institutions and universities that are increasing and will continue to do so in the coming months, whose list of names you can check in the following link:

Institutions and bodies that recognize OTE

The OTE examines the four competencies:

  • Reading
  • Listening
  • Speaking
  • Writing

In only 2 hours.

ADVANTAGES

  • The modules can be performed individually.
  • There is the possibility of repeating the modules individually and the report will always show the best will always show the best result obtained.
  • Test results are available in 14 days.
  • The report shows the results by skills and at a global level, according to the Common Framework levels European Reference (CEFR).

EXAM STRUCTURE

Understanding the Structure of Our Oxford Official Exams

SPEAKING

Oral expression. Verbal communication in English.

LISTENING

Listening. Listen to and understand spoken English.

READING

Reading comprehension. Read and understand texts in English.

WRITING

Written expression. Written communication in English.
