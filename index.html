COMMONLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find Answers to Your Queries

How can I enroll in a Cuatrovientos Idiomas course?

First of all, it is recommended that you take the online level test that is available on our website.
It is free and does not commit you to anything.
Registration is done online from our website by accessing the form through REGISTRATION and filling in the fields with your information. However, you can also access it by clicking HERE. Once you submit it, you will receive a copy by email, and your spot will be confirmed through email.

How many levels are there in the academy?

Our courses are adapted to the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages, and therefore the levels are:
  • A1, A2: Basic or Elementary level.
  • B1, B2: Intermediate level.
  • C1, C2: Advanced level.

Are there free trial sessions?

Without any commitment, you can come and try a first class completely FREE. Request it by clicking HERE.

Can I enroll at any time of the year?

Although it is highly recommended to enroll to start the course in October, you can enroll at any time of the year. If you wish to enroll after the course has started, it is advisable to take the level test beforehand, as mentioned before, to assign you to an appropriate group.

Do I have to start at the beginning of the month?

No. However, the monthly fee is paid in full, regardless of the day you join the classes.

How much do the courses cost and how is the payment made?

The payment will be made through DIRECT DEBIT from a bank account once the classes have started, taking into account that the amount varies depending on the specific course you enroll in and the chosen payment method from the following:
  • Monthly direct debit that will be charged at the beginning of each month (from October to May). It will be €75 or €50, depending on the chosen course.
  • Full course direct debit that will be charged at the beginning of October (with the discount already included). It will be €550 or €380, depending on the chosen course.

Are there any discounts or promotions?

If you pay the course in a single payment, you will receive a discount already included in the price.

Do I have to pay registration fee and textbooks or other materials?

There is no registration fee. As for textbooks, the teacher will indicate the materials to be used at the beginning of the course, and you will need to purchase them on your own.

Can I cancel my enrollment at any time?

There is no commitment or penalty; you can cancel your enrollment at any time, but please note that no refunds will be issued for any amount already paid. To request cancellation, you must NOTIFY us by sending an email to idiomas@cuatrovientos.org. The cancellation will be valid to avoid charging the direct debit ONLY if it is communicated BEFORE THE 25TH DAY OF THE PREVIOUS MONTH. Students who stop attending classes without notifying the cancellation as indicated will be responsible for the direct debit. If the direct debit is returned, it will remain pending for payment with any associated return fees. (Example: if I do not intend to attend classes in March, I must notify it to idiomas@cuatrovientos.org before February 25th) In the case of choosing the full course direct debit, if the student cancels the enrollment, there will be NO refund (UNLESS DULY JUSTIFIED FORCE MAJEURE REASONS).

How many students are there per group?

The number of students per class is between 8 and 14 students. The center reserves the right to cancel a started or unstarted course if it does not reach the minimum number of students. However, students will always be offered the opportunity to join another face-to-face or online group.

How is my progress evaluated?

Throughout the course, the teacher proposes different activities, tasks, and tests to monitor the teaching-learning process. The materials used in these courses also provide tools for self-evaluation of progress. At the end of the course, the teachers prepare a personalized report for each student, briefly discussing their achievements, areas for improvement, and the level attained.

What types of courses are available?

We offer general English and French courses, as well as courses for official exam preparation. We also offer English and French reinforcement courses specifically for students in vocational training programs. Most of our language courses are conducted in-person, but we also offer online training. If you are interested in online courses, we will provide you with more information.

When does the course start and end?

The course starts in October and ends in May. You can download the calendar by clicking HERE.

What is the objective of our courses?

The objective of our courses is to provide enjoyable and effective language training to our students so that they can achieve their personal goals by the end of the course, whether it is to improve their overall language skills or to obtain certification through an official exam.

Do these courses provide any official certification?

At the end of the course, the teachers prepare a personalized report indicating the achievements, areas for improvement, and the level attained. Any student can also request a certificate of achievement, which includes the number of hours invested during the course. However, please note that this certificate is not an official certification. All Cuatrovientos Idiomas (English) students have the opportunity to take an official exam at our center, as we are accredited as an Official OXFORD TEST OF ENGLISH Exam Center.

What if I want to prepare for an official exam?

The level test is very important to determine if you are ready to start a course for the specific exam you need. Our language teachers have extensive experience in preparing students for official exams, and they will help you decide if you are prepared for it. Cuatrovientos Idiomas is an official OXFORD exam center, and throughout the course, we organize different exam sessions open to the public. We also offer intensive preparatory courses so that candidates can become familiar with the exam format and how to approach it with the utmost confidence.

Who are our courses for?

Cuatrovientos Idiomas courses are designed for young people and adults who want to start or continue their learning of English or French. We also offer courses for those interested in preparing for an official language certification exam, as well as for students in vocational training programs, high school, or university who need to catch up in order to pass a subject or module at the end of the course. At Cuatrovientos Idiomas, we also offer customized courses for companies and professionals who need to improve their language skills in a workplace environment.
